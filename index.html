About

I am a research scientist at Google working at the intersection of cryptography, privacy, and machine learning. My work focuses on designing protocols and systems that allow useful computation over sensitive data.

Before joining Google, I was a researcher at The Alan Turing Institute and the University of Edinburgh, and a postdoc at SRI International. I completed my PhD at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya in Barcelona.

Research interests

  • Privacy-preserving secure distributed computations
  • Privacy and security in AI systems
  • Formal methods and program synthesis
  • AI for scientific discovery
